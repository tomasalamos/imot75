FAQs

Answers to questions you might have about Imot.

What does Imot do?

Imot is a smart data correction platform designed to clean, validate, and enhance industrial datasets. It helps teams eliminate noise, detect anomalies, and ensure data integrity for accurate analysis and decision-making.

Who is Imot for?

Imot is ideal for industrial companies, equipment operators, and data analysts who need to ensure the accuracy and reliability of their machine data.It's built for teams focused on optimizing operational efficiency by filtering, correcting, and analyzing equipment sensor data — helping prevent unplanned downtime, detect anomalies, and improve performance with confidence.

How secure is my data on Imot?

Security is our top priority. Imot processes all data locally in your browser, ensuring that sensitive information never leaves your device. No data is stored on our servers, keeping the platform completely secure and private. Additionally, we implement robust encryption methods for all communications and conduct regular security audits to ensure your data is protected at all times. To further enhance security, Imot offers role-based access control, allowing you to manage permissions within your team.

Is Imot easy to use if you're not technical?

Absolutely! Imot's interface is user-friendly and intuitive, designed for both technical and non-technical users. You can generate results without any coding knowledge. Just follow the septs!
